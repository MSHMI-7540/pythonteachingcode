Why KNOWYOURMEDS?

DID YOU KNOW Google receives more than 1 billion health-related questions every day? 45% have looked online for information about prescription or over-the-counter medications.

KNOWYOURMEDS helps to streamline and improve the process of finding information about over-the-counter medications, making it safer and more efficient.

Be Informed

KNOWYOURMEDS is designed to give you information about over-the-counter (OTC) medications.

You don't have to sift through irrelevant or poor-quality search results.

You can have more confidence in the information you receive and focus on medications that you can buy without a prescription.

Credible Sources

KNOWYOURMEDS uses data from OpenFDA and DailyMed.

OpenFDA empowers patients by providing access to information that they may not otherwise have.

The information on DailyMed is sourced from the FDA and represents the most recent submitted labeling by companies, ensuring patients have access to the latest information. .
